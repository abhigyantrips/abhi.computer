Turns out, the Astro CLI tries to greet you by your name/alias when you start a new project. I was pleasantly surprised when I saw it suddenly call me "Abhigyan", and went down a rabbit-hold to see why.

The specific code for it first checks your Git config, then runs a `whoami` exec, and finally falls back to "astronaut" as the default. What a cool human touch.
